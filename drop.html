Drop


It happens that a drop does not find a place to land,

as if it was nice knowing that you’re falling, as if it was right and normal.

It happens that a drop does not end up in the sea, does not lay on a flower and does not take a color.

The others are watching, crossed by the rays of the powerful sun,

sparkling with light and oscillating color, sitting at the end on a striped face,

They lie for a long time on a musky bed, wobbly sliding on a burning fireplace.

But she doesn't stop falling, what a shame, she doesn't want to land, she just can't accept it.

It happens that a drop does not find her place, she was maybe too light to get her feet on the ground.
